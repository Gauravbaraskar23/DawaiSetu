Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is placed, open "My Orders" to see its live status (Pending, Processing or Completed). You can chat directly with the Seller from the order page for any updates — DawaiSetu does not handle delivery itself, so the Seller is your point of contact for shipping and delivery questions.
Sellers can submit their FSSAI license for verification, after which they receive a blue verified badge on their storefront. We strongly recommend ordering only from verified sellers and checking their ratings before purchasing, since DawaiSetu is a marketplace and does not directly handle or inspect medicine stock.
Orders can only be cancelled by you while they are in the Pending state, directly from the order details page. Once the seller starts processing your order, cancellation is no longer available in-app — please contact them directly using the built-in Order Chat.
Order payment methods depend on the individual Seller's availability, such as Cash on Delivery, UPI or other options they support. Seller subscription plan payments (for store upgrades) are processed securely through Razorpay, supporting cards, UPI and net banking.
