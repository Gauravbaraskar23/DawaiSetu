Privacy Policy

At DawaiSetu, protecting your privacy and personal information is one of our highest priorities.

01

Data Collection

We collect essential information such as your name, email address, phone number, pharmacy details and delivery address to create your account, process medicine orders, communicate with sellers, and improve our platform experience.

02

Data Security

All user information, order details and conversations are protected using secure authentication and access controls. Only the buyer and the assigned seller can access their respective orders and chat history.

03

Information Sharing

Your personal information is never sold or rented to third parties. Contact and delivery information is shared only with the verified pharmacy responsible for fulfilling your order.

04

Cookies & Session Management

DawaiSetu may use browser cookies and secure sessions to keep you logged in, remember your preferences and provide a faster browsing experience without storing sensitive medical information inside cookies.

05

Policy Updates

Our Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, legal requirements or security enhancements. Significant changes will always be communicated through the platform.

Your Privacy Matters

We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity and security of your personal information. By using DawaiSetu, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
